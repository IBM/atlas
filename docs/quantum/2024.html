Strategic milestones

2024

Scalable error mitigation  brings unrivaled accuracy

In 2024, the accuracy of Qiskit Runtime will increase with error mitigation scaling to circuits of at least 100 qubits and depth 100. This will allow developers to derive better results from the output of quantum circuits.

Why this matters for our clients and the world

Qiskit Runtime with error mitigation will provide the foundation platform where algorithm and application developers can operate without worrying about the noise.

The technologies and innovations that will make this possible

Built-in error mitigation will automatically determine the best method to reduce the effect of noise. A prototype intelligent orchestration framework will allow to develop a compiler for classical and quantum workflows.

What platform or infrastructure will these advancements be delivered on

Qiskit Runtime service and exploratory devices will be available that scale the processor with modularity and a quantum communication network.
