Strategic milestones

2025

Multi-cloud, data-centric workloads become ubiquitous

By 2025, secure, multi-cloud, data-centric workloads will be ubiquitous. A digitized workforce with data-centric skills will raise the operational productivity of businesses by orders of magnitude.

Why this matters for our clients and the world

Enterprises will be able to prepare and access data products simply and securely, independent of location. The digitized workforce capabilities will expand to discovery, preparation, and consumption of data products.

The technologies and innovations that will make this possible

The data-centric skills for AI-based agents will be expanded to data composition, predictive modeling, and decision making. AI will be used to optimize where and how data is stored, moved, and transformed. Automated composition of the data stack and query components will make custom-fit solutions possible.

What platform or infrastructure will these advancements be delivered on

z/OS data on the cloud will have enhanced security with fully homomorphic encryption, multi-party computation, etc.
